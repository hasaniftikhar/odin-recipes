Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook pasta – Boil lasagna sheets until soft
  2. Make sauce – Cook meat or veggies with tomato sauce
  3. Layer – Alternate pasta, sauce, and cheese in a dish
  4. Bake – Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–40 minutes
