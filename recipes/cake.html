Chocolate Cake

Home

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix – Combine flour, cocoa, sugar, eggs, and butter
  2. Add milk – Mix in milk and baking powder to form smooth batter
  3. Pour – Pour the batter into a greased cake pan
  4. Bake – Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30–35 minutes
