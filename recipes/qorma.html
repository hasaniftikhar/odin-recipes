Ingredients

  • Meat (chicken or mutton)
  • Yogurt
  • Fried onions
  • Spices (garam masala, coriander, etc.)

Steps

  1. Fry onions – Golden fry sliced onions in oil
  2. Add meat – Cook meat with ginger-garlic paste and spices
  3. Mix yogurt – Stir in yogurt and let it simmer
  4. Cook – Cover and cook until meat is tender and oil separates
